Welcome to Ramanda Bakery

Where Every Bite is a Delight!

At Ramanda Bakery, we’re passionate about creating delicious moments that bring joy to your day. Located in the charming town of Ningthoukhong Kha-Khunou, our bakery is a haven for sweet lovers and food enthusiasts alike. From our freshly baked breads and delicate pastries to our custom cakes crafted for your special occasions, every treat is made with love and the finest ingredients.

Step inside and let the aroma of freshly baked goods envelop you, while our inviting atmosphere and friendly staff make you feel right at home. Whether you’re here to indulge in a quick snack or to order a centerpiece cake for a celebration, we’re dedicated to ensuring that every bite is a delightful experience. Join us on this sweet journey and discover the magic of Ramanda Bakery!
